Welcome to FixMyBike


Your one-stop solution for all bicycle repairs and maintenance.

🛠️ Our Services


  1. General Bike Service
    Complete checkup of your bike – includes engine oil check, air filter cleaning, chain lubrication, brake adjustment, and overall health check.
  2. Engine Oil Change
    We replace the old engine oil with high-quality oil to improve mileage, performance, and engine life.
  3. Brake Service
    Brake pad replacement, brake fluid top-up, and brake cable adjustment to ensure your safety on the road.
  4. Battery Check & Replacement
    We inspect your battery’s health and replace it if necessary, using trusted brands for reliability.
  5. Pickup & Drop Service
    Can’t visit the workshop? We’ll pick up your bike, service it, and drop it back at your doorstep.

  1. Puncture Repair
    On-site puncture repair for both tubeless and tube tyres. Quick and convenient.
  2. Chain & Sprocket Service
    Cleaning, lubrication, and tension adjustment to ensure a smooth and quiet ride.
  3. Water Wash & Polishing
    Professional bike wash with polishing – keeps your bike looking new and clean.
  4. Clutch & Accelerator Adjustment
    Smooth out the ride by adjusting clutch and accelerator cables as per standard tension.
  5. Electrical Checkup
    Inspection and repair of horn, lights, indicators, and wiring issues.
